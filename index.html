Chocolate Cake

"Be it any occasion, a Chocolate Cake never fails! It creates memories through its taste and flavours. Even if you are feeling low, this chocolate cake recipe will woo you over and make you feel happy as its moist and delicious."

Dificulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 35 min 45 min

Ingredients

250 grams all purpose flour

250 grams powdered sugar

4 Numbers egg beaten

2 tablespoon cocoa powder

1 teaspoon baking powder

Instractions

1

Mix all the dry ingredients and pre-heat the oven

2

Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients and bake the cake batter

3

Tasty Chocolate Cake is ready!

4

Garnish the cake with whipped cream and dark chocolate chips for an enhanced look.
